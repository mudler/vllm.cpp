## Scope

Add `LlamaForCausalLM` (Llama-3.2-1B: standard dense transformer — GQA 32/8, head_dim 64, SwiGLU, standard non-gemma RMSNorm, tied lm_head, `sliding_window` absent → one full-attention KV group) as the cross-family dense additivity proof: every model we support is a Qwen variant or OPT, and Llama is the mainstream dense arch. Llama IS the Qwen3-dense forward with exactly TWO deltas, both handled ADDITIVELY: (1) **NO per-head q/k RMSNorm** (Qwen3 has qk-norm; Llama does not) → the shared `dense_attn_block.h::AttnBlock` skips the norm step when q_norm/k_norm are empty; (2) **llama3 RoPE frequency scaling** (rope_theta=500000 + `rope_scaling` type `"llama3"`) → the one genuinely new primitive. Everything else (merged QKV, GQA, SwiGLU MLP, standard RMSNorm, tied lm_head, the full-attention paged forward) is REUSED VERBATIM. In scope: config consumption (already parsed+validated by `LoadHfConfig`), registry TU, full-attention-only KV spec, weight loader, the two shared-primitive generalizations (qk-norm-optional attn block, llama3 rope kernel), the SACRED token-exact gate. Out of scope: NVFP4/GGUF Llama, Llama-4/vision, sliding-window Llama variants, SPEED close (a separate increment).

## Risks/decisions

Decision: **Llama-3.2-1B** (head_dim 64) over 3B — 1B's generic-path head_dim-64 attention is CORRECTNESS-grade (the task's deliverable); 3B (head_dim 128) is the SPEED-increment vehicle (it can toggle the FA2 decode path). meta-llama is gated (401 on the weight download even with metadata access) → used the ungated `unsloth/Llama-3.2-1B` mirror (same weights, RECORDED). Risk: the `kLlama3` pretokenizer pattern was PROVISIONAL (no Llama-3 checkpoint existed when it was written) — verify our tokenization matches vLLM's on the battery, esp. BOS (Llama prepends 128000; the OPT bring-up added `EncodeWithSpecialTokens` for exactly this). Risk: llama3 rope scaling done in double (the kernels' existing angle precision) vs vLLM's fp32 inv_freq — negligible vs bf16 rounding, absorbed by the near-tie band; verified against the oracle. Numeric decision: rms_norm_eps 1e-5 (Llama) vs 1e-6 (Qwen3) flows from config.
